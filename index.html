About the program

Why we're doing this

Media and digital skills have become some of the most valuable things a young person can carry — for their own growth, and for how they serve in the work of God. A lot of teenagers in Lagos Province 7 already have the interest, they just haven't had structured guidance, mentorship or training. LP 7 Media Bootcamp is our first step toward closing that gap: five practical days that turn interest into real, usable skill.

How it runs

Everything happens virtually, so all you need is a smartphone, internet access and a willingness to show up.

  • YouTube — training videos and learning resources
  • WhatsApp — daily explanations, discussions, assignments and feedback
  • Telegram — submitting and comparing projects, plus PDFs and notes

Trainees who show strong commitment and excellence may go on to mentorship and recruitment into the Lagos Province 7 Media Department as active members.
